Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Finacify? expand_more

Finacify is an on-demand financial consultation platform where you can instantly connect with verified financial experts via chat, voice, or video for personalized advice on tax, investments, loans, and more.

2. How does the credit system work? expand_more

You purchase credits to book sessions with advisors. Each consultation deducts a set number of credits from your account. First-time users receive free credits to try the service.

3. Who are the financial experts on Finacify? expand_more

All experts undergo strict KYC verification and must submit valid licenses, certifications, and experience proofs before joining the platform.

4. What topics can I get advice on? expand_more

You can seek guidance on tax filing, budgeting, investment planning, loans, debts, business strategy, startup consultation, and more.

5. Is my data secure? expand_more

Yes. We use bank-level encryption, secure payment gateways, and Firebase authentication to protect your personal and financial information.

6. Can I remain anonymous during a consultation? expand_more

Yes. You can choose our voice-only anonymous consultation feature to discuss sensitive matters privately without revealing your identity.

7. How do I pay for consultations? expand_more

Payments can be made through UPI, debit/credit cards, or net banking. Razorpay powers our secure payment processing.

8. Can I choose my preferred language for consultations? expand_more

Yes. Advisors list the languages they speak, and you can filter experts by your preferred language before booking.

9. What if I’m not satisfied with the consultation? expand_more

You can rate and review advisors after each session. If an issue arises, our support team will review your case for a possible credit refund.

10. Do you offer an emergency financial helpline? expand_more

Yes. Our Emergency Financial Helpline connects you to an available advisor for urgent debt, fraud, or financial crisis guidance.

11. Are consultations recorded? expand_more

For your security and record-keeping, all sessions are securely recorded and can be accessed from your dashboard.

12. How do I top up my credits? expand_more

You can easily buy credit packages from the "Top Up Credits" section in your profile. Payments are securely processed through Razorpay.
